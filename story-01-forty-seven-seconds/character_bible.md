## 1. C1 — DANIEL VANCE (Narrator)

**One line:** A man paid to trust recordings more than memory, slowly discovering the recordings have stopped trusting *him*.

- **Age / role:** 34. Senior footage-restoration archivist at a media-preservation lab (digitises and repairs old tape, film, CCTV). Spends his life staring at frames for flaws — which is *why* he notices the gap, and why we believe him when he's fooled.
- **Appearance:** lean, slightly stooped from desk work; tired grey-green eyes; three-day stubble that becomes a beard as the story degrades; a small scar through one eyebrow; hands always faintly ink/dust-stained.
- **Clothing:** muted, practical — charcoal henley, open flannel, worn canvas jacket, lanyard with a lab keycard; an old wristwatch he trusts more than his phone (it, too, will lose 47 seconds).
- **Personality:** methodical, skeptical, quietly arrogant about his own perception; the kind of professional who says *"cameras don't lie."* That certainty is the wound the story opens.
- **Emotional arc:** Act I **rational/dismissive** → Act II **unsettled, over-documenting** → Act III **isolated, paranoid (rightly?)** → Act IV **hollowed-out, calm in a wrong way** — by the end he narrates the way the gaps feel: clipped, missing connective tissue.
- **Behaviour rule:** he never *reacts* to a gap in the moment (he can't — he wasn't there); he only reacts to the *evidence* of it, later, alone. This keeps the horror forensic.
- **The withheld fact (Act IV):** he has chased a 47-second gap once before — on someone else's footage, years ago. He never says what happened to them. (See F1.)
- **Visual seed (for AI files):** `DanielVance_34_lean_greygreen-eyes_stubble-to-beard_charcoal-henley_flannel_canvas-jacket_lanyard_old-wristwatch` — keep identical across all shots; beard length is the only thing that grows act to act.
- **Voice seed:** mid-low, dry, precise; an editor's cadence; flattens as the story progresses.

## 2. C2 — DR. MARA OKONKWO (Medical champion)

- **Age / role:** 50s. Neurologist; calm, evidence-first, kind but unsentimental.
- **Appearance:** silver-streaked locs, rimless glasses, navy clinic coat over a warm sweater; steady hands.
- **Clothing:** clinical, understated; a single thin gold ring.
- **Personality:** the voice of the *most comforting* explanation — and therefore the most seductive. She offers Daniel a way out: *"You are not haunted. You are unwell."*
- **Function:** delivers exhibit **X2** (the EEG with a 47-second flatline notation the technician "doesn't remember typing"). Her theory cannot explain E2/E3 on *other people's* devices — her fatal gap.
- **Behaviour rule:** never spooked; reframes every anomaly as symptom. Her calm becomes its own kind of dread.
- **Visual seed:** `DrOkonkwo_50s_silver-locs_rimless-glasses_navy-coat_warm-sweater_clinic`.

## 3. C3 — THEO BRANDT (Criminal champion / suspect)

- **Age / role:** 38. Former senior archivist at the same lab; laid off three months before Day 0; knew the systems better than anyone.
- **Appearance:** broad, restless, a permanent half-smile that doesn't reach the eyes; bitten nails; always slightly too close when he talks.
- **Clothing:** expensive-but-aging jacket, a lab keycard he technically shouldn't still have.
- **Personality:** charming, aggrieved, plausible. Could be a wronged man warning Daniel — or the man editing him.
- **Function:** linked to exhibit **X1** (the deleted user "—" active during three gaps; footprints in the blind corridor). Motive: bitterness, or a crime committed inside the gaps. His fatal gap: he can't fake **E2's** pre-event timestamp on a sealed cloud.
- **Behaviour rule:** appears *after* gaps, never during; always has an alibi that is 47 seconds too clean.
- **Visual seed:** `TheoBrandt_38_broad_restless_half-smile_aging-jacket_keycard`.

## 4. C4 & C5 — THE CONTRADICTORY WITNESSES

These two exist to make **Act III's social anomaly** land. They witnessed the *same* 47 seconds and remember it incompatibly. The story must never flag which is wrong.

**C4 — Priya Nair**, 29, colourist. Warm, certain, detail-oriented. Remembers Daniel *sitting at his desk the whole time, talking to her.*
- **Visual seed:** `PriyaNair_29_colourist_warm_box-braids_mustard-cardigan`.

**C5 — Sam Holloway**, 41, audio engineer. Dry, certain, equally credible. Remembers Daniel *leaving the room entirely and the lights going out.*
- **Visual seed:** `SamHolloway_41_audio-engineer_dry_shaved-head_headphones-around-neck_grey-tee`.

- **Behaviour rule (both):** each grows quietly cold toward Daniel after the gap they shared — not hostile, just *done with him*, as if some instinct flagged him. Neither can say why.

## 5. C6 — LENA VANCE (Emotional anchor)

- **Age / role:** 31. Daniel's younger sister; the one person whose warmth lets us *measure* how everyone else goes cold.
- **Appearance:** brighter palette than the film around her (deliberate — she's the last warm thing); same grey-green family eyes.
- **Clothing:** soft, lived-in; a scarf that recurs in a reflection near the Door (a planted detail for Reddit).
- **Personality:** loving, stubborn, refuses to accept the "medical" surrender on Daniel's behalf.
- **Function:** the stakes. In Act III she experiences a shared gap with Daniel — and afterward, for the first time, *she* hesitates before she hugs him. That hesitation is the story's gut-punch.
- **Visual seed:** `LenaVance_31_warm-palette_grey-green-eyes_soft-knit_recurring-scarf`.

## 6. THE ABSENCES (Anti-Characters — never shown plainly)

### A1 — The Deleted User "—"
A system account with no name (renders as a dash), created and deleted on the same second, **active during three gaps**. It has *access* to everything Daniel does. It is never a person on screen — only a row in a log, a permission that shouldn't exist. **Rule:** it is always *already gone* by the time he looks.

### A2 — The Breath
The off-frame exhale in exhibit **E4**, nine seconds into the captured gap. **Rules:** never sourced, never shown, never explained; whose it is must stay one of the four open readings. In audio files it is a *single* breath, close-mic, slightly out of sync with the room tone.

### A3 — The Incomplete Door
The recurring universe symbol (see lore §4): a too-narrow door, ajar, onto black, found only in the margins of evidence, usually near **2:47** / a **47**. **Rules:** grows by degree (a sliver in Act I → a full doorway in Act IV); never the subject of a shot; never walked through on screen.

## 7. F1 — THE EARLIER SUBJECT (Franchise seed — referenced only)

The person whose footage Daniel cleaned years ago, when he first met a 47-second gap. **Never named, never described, never resolved here.** One line late in the story implies Daniel knows exactly how this ends because he watched it happen to someone else — and did nothing. Reserved for a future story in the line.

## 8. CONSISTENCY GUIDE (for all later image/video/animation files)

- **Palette discipline:** the whole world is cold/desaturated **except Lena** (warm). The Door scenes pull *all* warmth out.
- **Daniel is the only constant face;** beard length = the only act-marker. Lock his seed everywhere.
- **The absences (A1–A3) must never be given a face or body.** If a prompt risks rendering a figure, render *negative space, a redaction bar, a reflection, or an open dark doorway* instead.
- **The recurring 47 / 2:47** should appear on a clock, a timecode, a file length, or a door/locker number in **every** evidence scene — subtle, never captioned.
- **Behaviour continuity:** people are normal *before* a shared gap and quietly cold *after.* Never a big reaction — a small withdrawal.
